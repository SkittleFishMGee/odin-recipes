The World's Best Lasagna

Lasagna is a good food, this is how you make it, OK?

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Assorted Italian Cheeses

How to Make It

  1. Preheat the Oven to 450 degrees
  2. Place the Lasagna Noodles in the boiling water
  3. Prepare the Tomato sauce by adding the garlic, crushed tomatoes, water, and beef into a saucepan
  4. Layer the noodles, sauce, and cheese on top of each other in a casserole dish.
  5. Once the lasagna is constructed, place it in the oven (preheated to 450) for 30 minutes, or until the dish is properly heated and the cheese is melted
