Oven Roasted Vegetables

It turns out vegetables taste better if you put them in the oven for a little bit with some other stuff. This is how you do it.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • 1 Onion
  • Broccoli
  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
  2. Cut up vegetables and place them on a baking sheet lined with Aluminum foil
  3. Drizzle olive oil over them, equally coating the vegetables
  4. Evenly apply saly and pepper to the vegetables
  5. Place the baking sheet in the oven
  6. Remove after 20-25 minutes of roasting
