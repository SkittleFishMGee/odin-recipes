Chicken Fried Rice

Have you ever asked yourseelf the question: Did a Shrimp fry this rice? The answer is no. You are going to fry the rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken Breast
  • One Onion
  • Spring Onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Sesame Oil

How to Make it

  1. Heat your pan
  2. Cook chicken and remove from pan
  3. Add Sesame Oil and allow it to heat up
  4. Add the Onion, spirng onion, and frozen carrots and peas and saute for 3-4 minutes or until tender
  5. Add the cold rice and saute evenly to toast it evenly
  6. Add the soy sauce and cooked Chicken
